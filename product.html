NOW BOARDING IN FIRST CLASS

Legendary only begins to describe the Douglas DC-3. In the 1930s, it luxuriously owned the open sky and gained popularity among the elite, offering the finest amenities and service money could buy. A trip onboard was a highly anticipated affair and every flight had an air of elegant exclusivity that is lost in today’s fast-paced routines. In all its ways, the Douglas DC-3 gave the world a finer perspective on travel, and so, defined the Golden Age of Aviation. This same spirit of vintage sophistication sparked inspiration for the AVIATOR Douglas Day-Date 41 timepiece, and was our muse for reviving the next Golden Era in watchmaking history.

HEADLINE NUMBER ONE

LUXURIOUSLY PATTERNED DIAL

The AVIATOR Douglas Day Date 41 dial features a subtle 3D pattern with volumetric markings and an elegantly curved window indicating the day. Since visibility and readability are key attributes, the Douglas Day Date 41 collection unites Art Deco design and function – from bold perfectly polished Roman numerals to the two elevated instrumental rings with luminous indexes that are inspired from the cockpit. With its deep and fine grooves, the pattern of the dial recreates stunning color reflections reminiscent to metal silk effect and symbolize a timeless sophistication.
